[Know Japan]

A land where natural splendours and timeless traditions meet neon sci-fi cities and a frenetic buzz, Japan invites discovery at every turn. Be inspired by authentic experiences, invigorating nature breaks and offbeat adventures doubling as refreshing escapes. Once you’re tapped out exploring, relive the day’s highlights in a cosy ryokan or plush suite—sure to supercharge your well-being. Japan intrigues with its kaleidoscope of sights and sounds. While wandering in the lap of luxury, make the most out of your next getaway and tick off as many once-in-a-lifetime experiences. Prepare to be enthralled, captivated and delighted all at once.

[Best Places]

Tokyo

As Japan's bustling capital, Tokyo is a dynamic mix of tradition and modernity. It's famous for attractions like the Tokyo Tower, Shibuya Crossing, and Sensoji Temple. The city is also known for its shopping districts (e.g., Shinjuku, Ginza), vibrant nightlife, and world-class dining.

Kyoto

Kyoto is the heart of Japan’s cultural heritage, renowned for its temples, shrines, and traditional tea houses. Highlights include the Fushimi Inari Shrine (with its iconic red torii gates), the Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion), and the Arashiyama Bamboo Grove. It’s a city rich in history and ancient traditions.

Osaka

Known for its food scene and friendly locals, Osaka is often called Japan's "kitchen." Tourists flock to Dotonbori for street food, neon lights, and canal-side restaurants. Other attractions include Osaka Castle, Universal Studios Japan, and the lively Shinsekai district.

Yokohama

Yokohama, Japan's second-largest city, features a stunning waterfront and a blend of modern and traditional attractions. Highlights include the vibrant Minato Mirai district, historic Chinatown, and beautiful parks. Just a short trip from Tokyo, it's perfect for both day trips and longer stays!

Hiroshima

Known worldwide for its history, Hiroshima is home to the Peace Memorial Park and the Atomic Bomb Dome, both dedicated to promoting peace. Nearby, the island of Miyajima is famous for the Itsukushima Shrine with its floating torii gate.
